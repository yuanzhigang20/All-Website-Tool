Formula

BMR = 10×kg + 6.25×cm − 5×age + sex adjustment

BMR versus daily calories

BMR estimates the energy your body uses at rest. Daily calorie needs are usually higher because they include walking, exercise, work, and normal movement. After calculating BMR, use the calorie calculator to apply an activity level and estimate maintenance calories.
