Formula

BMI categories for adults

BMI is a screening estimate, not a diagnosis. Common adult categories are under 18.5 for underweight, 18.5 to 24.9 for healthy weight, 25 to 29.9 for overweight, and 30 or higher for obesity. Athletes, children, older adults, and pregnant people should use professional guidance instead of relying on BMI alone.
