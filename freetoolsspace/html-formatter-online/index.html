FAQ

Does this validate HTML?

It formats markup for readability. It is not a full standards validator.

Will it change my code?

It may adjust whitespace between tags, but it does not intentionally rewrite tag names or attributes.

Can I minify the output?

This page focuses on readable formatting. You can remove extra whitespace manually if you need a compact snippet.

Is it private?

Yes, the formatting runs locally in your browser.
