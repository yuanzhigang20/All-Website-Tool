Slug generator tips

Good slugs are short, descriptive, and stable. They should include the main page keyword but avoid dates, filler words, and punctuation unless those details are essential to the page.

This tool is useful when planning blog posts, tool pages, landing pages, and product URLs. For bulk work, paste multiple titles on separate lines and copy the generated list.

FAQ

What is a URL slug?

A slug is the readable part of a URL that identifies a page, usually after the domain name.

What makes a slug SEO friendly?

Short lowercase words, hyphens, clear keywords, and no unnecessary symbols usually work best.

Can I generate slugs in bulk?

Yes. Put one title per line and the tool creates one slug per line.

Should I change old slugs?

Only with care. Changing existing URLs can require redirects to avoid losing traffic.
