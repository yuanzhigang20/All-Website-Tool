FAQ

What counts as a word?

The tool counts groups of letters or numbers separated by spaces or punctuation as words.

Does it count spaces in characters?

Yes. It shows characters with spaces and characters without spaces.

Can I estimate reading time?

Yes. Reading time is estimated at about 200 words per minute.

Is pasted text private?

Yes. Counts are calculated in your browser and are not uploaded by this static page.
